Jamie’s Italian historical Story.


Jamie’s Italian was founded by Jamie Oliver and his Italian mentor, chef Gennaro Contaldo, in Oxford, 2008. Since then it has grown to more than 60 restaurants worldwide, with plans for even more in towns, cities and countries around the world.

Moreover, Jamie’s Italian started with what Italian’s are most proud of – fantastic, rustic dishes, using recipes that have been tested, tried and loved. Our menu is rooted in authenticity which is driven by the food commonly enjoyed by Italian individuals all over Italy.

Taking inspiration from the “Italian table” – where people relax, share and enjoy each other’s company – our menu is full of antipasti and nibbles, perfect for sharing, as well as comforting bowls of pasta, beautiful fresh salads and grills washed down with thirst quenching beverages.

Return On Investment.

Our venue is promoted and marketed to the world through three social media platforms, Instagram, Twitter and Facebook. Each social media platform is monitored regularly by our marketing manager, Leanne Van Wyk.

Our Facebook page is more mature and refined in its approach as the majority of the older crowd, who have become daily customers of Jamie’s Italian, are generally more active on Facebook. On the other hand, our Instagram page is more toned and high-ended, as our venue uses the platform to target the young and classy individuals that are food bloggers or Instagram socialites/celebrities.

Whereas, our Twitter page has a far more extensive reach as it appeals to the older and the young generation that make use of the platform. The important aspect is that on all of the Jamie’s Italian social media pages we aim to expose the various, delicious and beautifully created dishes to a vast majority of individuals across all age groups therefore allowing a discerning crowd to choose Jamie’s Italian as their number one restaurant choice
